# Code of conduct

We want this project to be useful to investors, researchers, engineers, and newcomers with different levels of market and programming experience.

Be respectful, discuss evidence instead of attacking people, disclose uncertainty, and give contributors room to correct mistakes. Harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, spam, market manipulation, and promotion disguised as technical contribution are not acceptable.

Maintainers may edit or remove content that violates these expectations and may restrict participation when necessary. Report conduct concerns through a private maintainer channel when one is available; do not expose personal information in a public Issue.
